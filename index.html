Introduction

Jinhua, located in the central part of Zhejiang Province, is a city with a long history and rich culture. It's renowned for its numerous historical sites that tell stories of the past eras.
The city is blessed with beautiful natural landscapes. From the meandering rivers to the gentle hills, every corner presents a picturesque view. The Yiwu River flowing through Jinhua adds a touch of vitality to the whole city, and people can often be seen taking a stroll along its banks, enjoying the peaceful atmosphere.
Jinhua is also famous for its unique local cuisine. The delicious Jinhua ham, with its distinct flavor and tender texture, has won the hearts of food lovers both at home and abroad. Besides, there are various snacks in the streets and alleys, waiting for people to explore and taste.
